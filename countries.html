Over the years, we have continued to build relationships with organizations, activists, churches, youth groups, and human rights defenders who are committed to creating safer, more inclusive, and more just communities.

Today, our work and partnerships extend across several countries, including Burundi, Mozambique, Zambia, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Uganda, Kenya, Rwanda, Sudan, and Afghanistan. Through these collaborations, we continue supporting vulnerable and marginalized communities facing discrimination, violence, inequality, conflict, and human rights violations.

Our mission focuses on promoting human rights, mental health and psychosocial support, youth empowerment, advocacy, education, peacebuilding, and community development. In many of the countries where we work, communities continue to face social exclusion, economic hardship, insecurity, and limited access to support services. By partnering with local organizations and community leaders, we are able to strengthen grassroots initiatives and create sustainable impact.

Strengthening Partnerships for Greater Impact

As HGY Mission continues to grow, we are actively seeking to establish new partnerships with organizations and institutions that share our vision of dignity, equality, justice, and community empowerment. We believe that partnerships are essential in addressing today's social and humanitarian challenges. Working together allows us to share knowledge, strengthen advocacy efforts, mobilize resources, and expand support to communities that need it most.

Collaboration

How Potential Partners Can Work With Us

01 — Program Collaboration
Community Projects & Campaigns
Organizations can partner with HGY Mission on community projects, awareness campaigns, advocacy programs, conferences, workshops, youth activities, and humanitarian initiatives.
02 — Advocacy
Joint Human Rights Work
Partners can collaborate to raise awareness about discrimination, violence, inequality, and the protection of vulnerable communities through campaigns, public engagement, and policy advocacy.
03 — Capacity Building
Training & Leadership
We work with partners to strengthen leadership, community organizing, advocacy skills, volunteer development, and organizational capacity.
04 — Outreach
Community Education
Churches, CSOs, and human rights organizations can join us in conducting community education programs, mental health awareness sessions, peacebuilding activities, and youth empowerment initiatives.
05 — Research
Documentation & Storytelling
Partners can support research, storytelling, monitoring, and documentation of community challenges and human rights concerns to strengthen evidence-based advocacy.
06 — Emergency Response
Humanitarian Support
In areas affected by conflict, displacement, or persecution, partnerships help us provide psychosocial support, referrals, emergency response, and community protection services.
Get Involved

How to Become a Partner

Organizations interested in partnering with HGY Mission can begin by contacting us and sharing the following information. Following initial discussions, we identify shared goals and explore opportunities based on mutual respect, transparency, accountability, and shared impact.

  • A brief introduction of your organization
  • Mission and areas of work
  • Countries or communities served
  • Possible areas of collaboration
  • Previous or ongoing projects
  • Contact information for representatives
