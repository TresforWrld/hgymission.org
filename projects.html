Our Projects

Responding to Urgent Needs

HGY Mission implements a range of projects designed to respond to urgent needs while promoting long-term resilience and empowerment.

Relocation

Relocation of Harassed Activists

We assist activists, human rights defenders, and individuals facing persecution or security threats by facilitating safe relocation, emergency shelter, and transition support to protect their lives and dignity.

Legal Aid

Legal Aid and Rights

Legal aid projects provide access to legal guidance, documentation support, rights awareness, referrals, and representation for vulnerable individuals facing injustice, discrimination, or abuse.

Mentorship

Mentorship Programs

Mentorship programs create pathways for leadership development, confidence building, and long-term social participation for young people and emerging community leaders.

Education

Education

Education projects support access to learning, literacy, and skills development for children, youth, and adults in underserved and conflict-affected communities.

GBV Response

Gender Based Violence Interventions

Our GBV interventions focus on survivor-centred support through protection referrals, psychosocial care, safe spaces, and access to recovery services for those affected by violence.

Empowerment

Economic Empowerment

Economic empowerment projects help women, youth, and vulnerable households build livelihoods through entrepreneurship support, vocational skills, financial literacy, and income generating opportunities.

Mental Health

Psychosocial Support

We provide psychosocial support and mental health services to individuals and communities experiencing trauma, displacement, violence, or chronic stress in crisis-affected settings.

Peacebuilding

Peacebuilding & Reconciliation

Peacebuilding projects bring divided communities together through dialogue, reconciliation processes, and conflict resolution programmes in post-conflict and fragile settings.

Advocacy

Human Rights Advocacy

We advocate for the rights of marginalized and persecuted communities, raising awareness and engaging decision-makers to drive policy change and accountability.

Our Approach

How We Deliver Impact

Community Led
Projects are designed with and led by the communities they serve, ensuring local ownership and relevance.
Protection First
Safety and dignity of vulnerable individuals are at the core of every intervention we implement.
Evidence Based
Data and community feedback drive our decisions, ensuring resources go where they are needed most.
Sustainable
We build for lasting change, not quick fixes, ensuring communities thrive long after projects end.
