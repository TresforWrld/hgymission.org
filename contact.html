Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I partner with HGY Mission?
We welcome partnerships with churches, schools, civil society organisations, and government bodies. Simply reach out through the form above or email us at info@hgymission.org and our partnerships team will respond within two business days.
Can I volunteer with HGY Mission?
Yes, we welcome volunteers in various capacities including community outreach, legal aid support, mentorship, and event coordination. Select the volunteer option in the form above and our team will guide you through the process.
What countries does HGY Mission operate in?
We currently operate across nine African countries: Zimbabwe, Zambia, Malawi, Mozambique, Burundi, Nigeria, Ghana, Somalia, and Sudan. Visit our Countries page to learn more about our work in each location.
How is my donation used?
All donations go directly toward supporting our programmes in legal aid, protection, empowerment, and community resilience. We maintain full financial transparency and provide annual impact reports to our donors.
I need urgent help. What should I do?
If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services first. For urgent support from HGY Mission, use the emergency email below and our team will respond as quickly as possible.
