Gatherings

Conferences and Annual Gatherings

HGY Mission convenes annual conferences, regional dialogues, strategic consultations, and leadership gatherings that bring together partners, activists, faith leaders, survivors, youth representatives, and development actors.

Our Gatherings

Spaces for Reflection and Collaboration

Annual Conference

Annual Conferences

Annual conferences serve as important moments to review impact, share experiences, highlight emerging challenges, and build stronger collective responses for the future. These gatherings bring together all stakeholders for strategic reflection and planning.

Regional Dialogue

Regional Dialogues

Regional dialogues provide space for reflection, learning, policy discussion, partnership strengthening, and collaborative planning across different geographic areas and contexts where HGY Mission operates.

Strategic

Strategic Consultations

Strategic consultations bring together key decision makers, partners, and experts to assess programme effectiveness, explore new approaches, and align organisational strategy with evolving community needs.

Leadership

Leadership Gatherings

Leadership gatherings create opportunities for cross country exchange, allowing participants from different regions to learn from each other and strengthen networks that advance justice and community resilience.

Impact

What Our Gatherings Deliver

Shared Learning
Participants gain new insights, best practices, and innovative approaches from peers working in similar contexts across the continent.
Network Building
Gatherings strengthen relationships between partners, activists, and faith leaders, creating lasting networks for collaboration and mutual support.
Collective Strategy
Collaborative planning sessions produce aligned strategies, joint advocacy positions, and coordinated responses to emerging challenges.
Cross Country Exchange

Learning Across Borders

Our gatherings also create opportunities for cross country exchange, allowing participants from different regions to learn from each other and strengthen networks that advance justice and community resilience. These exchanges build solidarity, share practical solutions, and foster a sense of shared purpose that transcends national boundaries.
