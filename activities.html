In Action

Trans Goals Into Practical Action

Our activities translate project goals into practical action within communities. HGY Mission regularly conducts community outreach sessions, legal awareness meetings, psychosocial support sessions, training workshops, leadership seminars, and livelihood coaching programs.

Community Engagement

What We Do on the Ground

Community Outreach
Regular outreach sessions bring vital information, support services, and resources directly to communities that need them most.
Training Workshops
Capacity building sessions for community leaders, churches, activists, and local organisations strengthen protection systems and improve referral pathways.
Advocacy Dialogues
Public awareness campaigns, youth engagement forums, and women empowerment circles form the backbone of our ongoing advocacy activities.
Our Activities

From Goals to Ground Level Action

We organise capacity building sessions for community leaders, churches, activists, and local organisations to strengthen protection systems and improve referral pathways. Advocacy dialogues, public awareness campaigns, youth engagement forums, and women empowerment circles are also part of our ongoing activities.

Activity Areas

Key Areas of Engagement

Community
Outreach Sessions
Regular community outreach sessions bring information about rights, services, and support directly to vulnerable populations in both urban and rural settings.
Legal
Legal Awareness Meetings
Legal awareness meetings educate communities about their rights, available legal protections, and how to access justice systems and documentation support.
Wellbeing
Psychosocial Support Sessions
Psychosocial support sessions provide safe spaces for healing, counselling, and group therapy for survivors of violence, displacement, and trauma.
Leadership
Leadership Seminars
Leadership seminars and livelihood coaching programs equip emerging community leaders with skills, confidence, and networks for long term social participation.
Empowerment
Women and Youth Forums
Women empowerment circles and youth engagement forums create platforms for voices that are often marginalised to be heard, respected, and acted upon.
Crisis Response

Emergency Response When It Matters Most

In crisis situations, we coordinate emergency response activities including distribution of relief support, urgent referrals, and protection interventions for individuals at immediate risk. Our networks allow us to respond rapidly when communities face sudden displacement, violence, natural disasters, or other emergencies.
