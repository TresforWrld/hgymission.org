Who We Are

A Faith Driven Mission for Justice and Dignity

HGY Mission is a faith driven human rights organisation fighting for the weak, the marginalised, and the defenceless across Africa. Founded on the conviction that every person bears the image of God, we work to advance justice, freedom, dignity, and inclusion for all.

Our Story

Rooted in Faith, Driven by Justice

HGY Mission was established with a clear calling: to stand alongside those who are most vulnerable and to challenge the systems that perpetuate suffering and exclusion. Our work is grounded in the belief that faith compels action, and that the pursuit of justice is not optional but essential to our spiritual witness.

From our earliest days working in local communities to our current operations across seven African countries, we have remained committed to building responses that are locally grounded while addressing broader regional concerns. We partner with churches, civil society organisations, legal practitioners, youth leaders, and grassroots activists who share our vision of a world where every person can live in freedom and dignity.

Our Values

What Guides Us

Compassion
We approach every person and community with empathy, kindness, and genuine care, recognising the inherent worth and dignity of all people.
Justice
We are committed to challenging injustice, advocating for the rights of the vulnerable, and working towards systems that protect and uplift every person.
Hope
We believe in the possibility of transformation and work tirelessly to build a future where every community can thrive in safety and freedom.
Community
We believe sustainable change happens when communities lead their own transformation, supported by networks of trust and solidarity.
Integrity
We hold ourselves to the highest standards of transparency, accountability, and ethical practice in everything we do and every relationship we build.
Faith
Our work is rooted in a deep faith that compels action, drives perseverance, and inspires hope even in the most challenging circumstances.
