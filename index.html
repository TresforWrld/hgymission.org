Our Mission

Ground them in values.
Lift them to their potential.

Roots & Wings believes that circumstance should never dictate destiny. We work in under-resourced communities to remove barriers to education, connect young people with transformative mentors, and build the confidence every child deserves.

Education Access
Scholarships, tutoring centres, and learning materials for every child in our network.
Mentorship Network
Over 2,400 vetted professionals who guide and champion our youth one-on-one.
Skills for Tomorrow
Digital literacy, coding, and entrepreneurship workshops preparing youth for the modern world.

Our Programs

View all

Roots Scholars

Full-cycle scholarship program covering tuition, books, transport, and meals for academically promising youth from households below the poverty line.

Learn more

Wings Mentorship

Structured 12-month one-on-one mentorship pairing young people aged 13–21 with experienced professionals, entrepreneurs, and community leaders.

Learn more

FutureForge

Intensive 8-week digital skills bootcamps covering web development, graphic design, and financial literacy — with job placement support on completion.

Learn more
